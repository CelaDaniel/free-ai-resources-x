# AI Security & Privacy

- [Google: Federated Learning](https://federated.withgoogle.com/) - Free resources and tutorials on privacy-preserving machine learning.
- [Adversarial Machine Learning Tutorial](https://adversarial-ml-tutorial.org/) - Free comprehensive guide to adversarial attacks and defenses.
- [OpenMined: Privacy-Preserving AI](https://courses.openmined.org/) - Free courses on federated learning and differential privacy.
- [Microsoft: Responsible AI Security](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/security/business/ai-machine-learning-security) - Free resources on secure AI development.
- [Coursera: Privacy in the Digital Age](https://www.coursera.org/learn/privacy-in-the-digital-age) - Free to audit, covers privacy-preserving techniques.
- [Differential Privacy Resources](https://differentialprivacy.org/resources/) - Free collection of papers, tutorials, and code on differential privacy.
