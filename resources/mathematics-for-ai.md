# Mathematics for AI

- [3Blue1Brown: Essence of Linear Algebra](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLZHQObOWTQDPD3MizzM2xVFitgF8hE_ab) - Free visual introduction to linear algebra concepts.
- [MIT OpenCourseWare: Linear Algebra](https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/18-06-linear-algebra-spring-2010/) - Free complete linear algebra course by Gilbert Strang.
- [Khan Academy: Statistics & Probability](https://www.khanacademy.org/math/statistics-probability) - Free comprehensive statistics and probability courses.
- [Mathematics for Machine Learning Book](https://mml-book.github.io/) - Free online textbook covering essential math for ML.
- [Coursera: Mathematics for Machine Learning Specialization](https://www.coursera.org/specializations/mathematics-machine-learning) - Free to audit, covers linear algebra, calculus, and PCA.
- [StatQuest (YouTube)](https://www.youtube.com/c/joshstarmer) - Free intuitive explanations of statistics and ML math concepts.
