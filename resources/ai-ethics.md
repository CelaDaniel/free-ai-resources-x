# AI Ethics

- [MIT: Ethics of AI](https://mit-serc.pubpub.org/ai-ethics) - Free course exploring ethical considerations in AI development and deployment.
- [Google: Responsible AI Practices](https://ai.google/responsibilities/responsible-ai-practices/) - Free guide on building AI responsibly.
- [Fast.ai: Practical Data Ethics](https://ethics.fast.ai/) - Free course on ethical implications of data science and AI.
- [Elements of AI: Ethics Module](https://www.elementsofai.com/) - Free section on AI ethics within comprehensive AI course.
- [Stanford HAI: AI Ethics Resources](https://hai.stanford.edu/policy-resources) - Free collection of research and resources on AI ethics and policy.
- [Partnership on AI: Resources](https://partnershiponai.org/resources/) - Free resources, case studies, and guidelines for responsible AI.
