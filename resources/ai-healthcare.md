# AI in Healthcare

- [Stanford: AI in Healthcare Specialization](https://www.coursera.org/specializations/ai-healthcare) - Free to audit, comprehensive course on healthcare AI applications.
- [fast.ai: Medical Imaging](https://www.fast.ai/) - Free practical deep learning with medical imaging examples.
- [DeepMind Health Research Papers](https://www.deepmind.com/research/highlighted-research/alphafold) - Free access to cutting-edge healthcare AI research.
- [MIT: Machine Learning for Healthcare](https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/6-s897-machine-learning-for-healthcare-spring-2019/) - Free MIT OpenCourseWare on ML in medicine.
- [Coursera: AI for Medical Diagnosis](https://www.coursera.org/learn/ai-for-medical-diagnosis) - Free to audit, course on diagnostic AI systems.
- [Kaggle: Healthcare Datasets & Competitions](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets?tags=13107-Health) - Free real-world healthcare datasets for practice.
