# Data Science

- [Harvard: Data Science: R Basics](https://online-learning.harvard.edu/course/data-science-r-basics) - Free, introductory data science course.
- [Kaggle: Intro to Data Science](https://www.kaggle.com/learn) - Free hands-on data science courses with real datasets.
- [IBM: Data Science Fundamentals](https://www.ibm.com/training/collection/data-science-fundamentals-1) - Free courses covering data science basics and tools.
- [Microsoft: Data Science for Beginners](https://github.com/microsoft/Data-Science-For-Beginners) - Free 20-lesson curriculum on data science fundamentals.
- [Google: Data Analytics Certificate Courses](https://grow.google/certificates/data-analytics/) - Free access to some modules on data analytics.
- [Johns Hopkins: Data Science Specialization](https://www.coursera.org/specializations/jhu-data-science) - Free to audit, comprehensive 10-course specialization.
