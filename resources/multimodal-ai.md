# Multimodal AI

- [CMU: Multimodal Machine Learning](https://cmu-multicomp-lab.github.io/mmml-course/fall2022/) - Free comprehensive course on multimodal learning from Carnegie Mellon.
- [OpenAI CLIP: Tutorial](https://github.com/openai/CLIP) - Free documentation and examples for vision-language models.
- [Hugging Face: Multimodal Models](https://huggingface.co/docs/transformers/main/en/model_doc/auto#multimodal-models) - Free guides on using multimodal transformers.
- [DeepMind: Flamingo & Multimodal Research](https://www.deepmind.com/blog/tackling-multiple-tasks-with-a-single-visual-language-model) - Free access to multimodal AI research papers.
- [Stanford: Vision and Language Course](https://web.stanford.edu/class/cs231n/) - Free materials on vision-language understanding.
- [Papers: Multimodal Learning](https://arxiv.org/list/cs.MM/recent) - Free access to latest multimodal AI research on arXiv.
