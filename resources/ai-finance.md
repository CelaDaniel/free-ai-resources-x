# AI in Finance

- [NYU: Machine Learning in Finance](https://www.coursera.org/learn/machine-learning-in-finance) - Free to audit, course on ML applications in finance.
- [QuantInsti: Quantitative Trading Strategies](https://quantra.quantinsti.com/courses) - Free courses on algorithmic trading and financial ML.
- [Coursera: Machine Learning for Trading](https://www.coursera.org/specializations/machine-learning-trading) - Free to audit, specialization on trading algorithms.
- [Microsoft: Finance Time Series Forecasting](https://github.com/microsoft/forecasting) - Free repository with financial forecasting tutorials.
- [Kaggle: Financial Datasets & Competitions](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets?tags=13302-Finance) - Free real-world financial datasets for practice.
- [Papers: Financial Machine Learning](https://papers.ssrn.com/sol3/Jeljour_results.cfm?form_name=journalBrowse&journal_id=3930309) - Free access to financial ML research papers.
