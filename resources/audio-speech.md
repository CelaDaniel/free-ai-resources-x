# Audio & Speech Processing

- [Hugging Face: Audio Course](https://huggingface.co/learn/audio-course/chapter0/introduction) - Free comprehensive course on audio processing with transformers.
- [Coursera: Audio Signal Processing for Music Applications](https://www.coursera.org/learn/audio-signal-processing) - Free to audit, covers audio analysis fundamentals.
- [Mozilla Common Voice](https://commonvoice.mozilla.org/en/datasets) - Free open speech dataset and tutorials.
- [AssemblyAI: Speech Recognition Tutorials](https://www.assemblyai.com/blog) - Free guides on speech-to-text and audio AI.
- [SpeechBrain Documentation](https://speechbrain.github.io/) - Free toolkit and tutorials for speech processing.
- [Python for Audio Signal Processing (YouTube)](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PL-wATfeyAMNqIee7cH3q1bh4QJFAaeNv0) - Free video series on audio ML.
