# AI Career & Interview Prep

- [Chip Huyen: Machine Learning Interviews Book](https://huyenchip.com/ml-interviews-book/) - Free comprehensive guide to ML interview preparation.
- [Kaggle: Getting Started Guide](https://www.kaggle.com/learn) - Free courses to build portfolio projects for AI careers.
- [AI/ML Interview Questions Repository](https://github.com/andrewekhalel/MLQuestions) - Free collection of ML interview questions and answers.
- [DeepLearning.AI: AI For Everyone](https://www.coursera.org/learn/ai-for-everyone) - Free course understanding AI career paths and opportunities.
- [Fast.ai: Practical Deep Learning](https://course.fast.ai/) - Free course to build portfolio-worthy projects.
- [LinkedIn Learning: AI Career Paths](https://www.linkedin.com/learning/paths/advance-your-career-in-ai) - Free trial access to AI career development courses.
