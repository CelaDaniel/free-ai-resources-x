# Time Series & Forecasting

- [Coursera: Sequences, Time Series and Prediction](https://www.coursera.org/learn/tensorflow-sequences-time-series-and-prediction) - Free to audit, TensorFlow-based time series course.
- [Kaggle: Time Series Course](https://www.kaggle.com/learn/time-series) - Free hands-on course for time series forecasting.
- [AWS: Forecasting with Amazon Forecast](https://aws.amazon.com/forecast/getting-started/) - Free tutorials on time series ML.
- [Time Series Analysis in Python](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLvcbYUQ5t0UHOLnBzl46_Q6QKtFgfMGc3) - Free YouTube series on time series with Python.
- [Prophet by Meta: Documentation](https://facebook.github.io/prophet/) - Free tool and tutorials for time series forecasting.
- [Nixtla: Time Series Forecasting](https://nixtla.github.io/statsforecast/) - Free open-source time series forecasting tools and guides.
