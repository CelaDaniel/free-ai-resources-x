# Robotics & Autonomous Systems

- [ETH Zurich: Autonomous Mobile Robots](https://ethz.ch/content/dam/ethz/special-interest/mavt/robotics-n-intelligent-systems/asl-dam/documents/lectures/AMR.pdf) - Free course materials on autonomous robotics and control.
- [ROS (Robot Operating System) Tutorials](https://wiki.ros.org/ROS/Tutorials) - Free comprehensive tutorials for the most popular robotics framework.
- [Coursera: Robotics Specialization by Penn](https://www.coursera.org/specializations/robotics) - Free to audit, comprehensive robotics fundamentals.
- [MIT: Autonomous Vehicles](https://selfdrivingcars.mit.edu/) - Free course on deep learning for self-driving cars.
- [edX: Autonomous Mobile Robots](https://www.edx.org/learn/robotics/eth-zurich-autonomous-mobile-robots) - Free robotics course from ETH Zurich.
- [The Construct: ROS Tutorials](https://www.theconstructsim.com/ros-courses-library/) - Free ROS learning resources and simulations.
